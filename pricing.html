Pricing is Simple and Straightforward

Our pricing is simple by design. Membership provides direct access to your doctor without the complexity of insurance, while each visit is billed at a clear, flat rate based on your time with the physician. (Don't worry, your time won't be wasted in waiting rooms.) This keeps costs fair for patients who only need occasional care, while still predictable for those who require more frequent visits. With no hidden fees, surprise bills, or third-party interference, you’ll always know exactly what you’re paying for, allowing you to focus on your health instead of navigating insurance red tape.
