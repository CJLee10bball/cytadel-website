CYTADEL Health is a direct primary care clinic based in Austin, Texas with the mission to build a parallel healthcare system that puts patients and physicians at the center. By removing unnecessary middlemen and using transparent pricing, we restore medicine to what it was meant to be: a direct, productive relationship rooted in trust and shared goals.

Through an innovative direct payment model drawing inspiration from Singapore’s world-leading healthcare system, CYTADEL aligns incentives to connect patients with physicians who are genuinely invested in helping them achieve their goals. Gone are the hurried office visits where you don’t feel heard. Gone are the rushed medication prescriptions without any discussion of what’s really going on. In their place, a provider who takes time to listen, build a personalized health plan, and walk with you every step of the way. Whether you focus on managing a chronic condition, optimizing performance, or preventing future illness, we’re here to help.

No gatekeepers. No gimmicks. Just you, your doctor, and a plan that works for your life, your values, and your future.

Our approach uses free market principles to deliver better care and better outcomes. A simple monthly membership secures your place in the practice and ensures you have a physician who knows your history and goals. When you need care, whether for a comprehensive annual health review, follow-up, or urgent issue, you pay a straightforward flat fee with prices that are always clear. Membership provides same- or next-day appointments, telemedicine, extended visits, preventive care, and chronic disease management, along with direct communication and no call center barriers. For labs, imaging, or specialty referrals, CYTADEL negotiates affordable rates so you have full visibility and control. The result is timely access, predictable costs, and returning the doctor-patient relationship to the center of medicine.
