Our Mission

MEDICINE THE WAY IT SHOULD BE

The modern healthcare system has separated doctors from patients, buried both in bureaucracy, and optimized for billing codes instead of outcomes. CYTADEL is the antidote, a practice built entirely on your needs, your goals, and your long-term health.

We practice medicine from first principles. That means asking why and tracing symptoms to their source, not masking them. It means time with your doctor, not a 7-minute visit. And it means a relationship built on trust, transparency, and your full autonomy.

01

First Principles

We understand disease at its root: biology, behavior, environment, and genetics.

02

Patient Autonomy

You make the decisions. We give you the knowledge and tools to make them wisely.

03

Do No Harm

The Hippocratic Oath isn't a formality here, it's the foundation of every choice.

04

Results-Based

We measure success by your health outcomes, not by how many patients we churn.

Why CYTADEL

THREE REASONS PATIENTS CHOOSE US

We've rebuilt the practice from the ground up around what actually matters in medicine.

Transparent Pricing

Every cost is disclosed upfront. No coding tricks, no facility fees, no mystery EOBs. What you see is what you pay — always. We believe financial clarity is part of the Hippocratic Oath.

Areas of Care

YOUR GOALS. OUR FOCUS.

CYTADEL serves patients across the full spectrum of health goals. Tell us where you want to be.

Performance

For athletes, executives, and high-performers seeking a biological edge — optimized recovery, hormones, VO₂ max, sleep, and cognitive function.

Longevity

Proactive aging protocols grounded in the science of healthspan — metabolic health, cardiovascular risk, inflammation, and the hallmarks of aging.

Prevention

Catching disease before it starts. Deep screening, lifestyle medicine, and risk stratification for patients who refuse to wait until something goes wrong.

Disease

Treating chronic and complex conditions with depth — root cause analysis instead of symptom suppression, and care plans that evolve with you.

Founder & Physician
Dr. Lee
MD  ·  Board Certified  ·  Austin, TX

Dr. Lee earned his MD from Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center and completed his Family Medicine residency at the University of Florida, where he also earned an MBA. During his training he developed a strong interest in sports medicine, which evolved into a broader focus on optimizing performance and long-term health.

He approaches medicine from first principles, using advanced diagnostics, detailed clinical history, and clear conversations about goals to build individualized plans for each patient.

Dr. Lee has also studied firsthand how different cultures approach diet, movement, and longevity. These perspectives shape a practice philosophy that combines rigorous evidence-based medicine with a broader understanding of how people live and perform.

Outside the clinic, he enjoys basketball and strength training and brings the same performance mindset to helping patients pursue stronger and healthier lives.
